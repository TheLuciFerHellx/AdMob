Here is the simple, step-by-step setup to put an AdMob ad on **any button**:

### Step 1: Create the Ad Manager (Do this once)
1. Create an empty GameObject in your main menu scene and name it `AdMobManager`.
2. Attach the **`AdMobManager.cs`** script to it.

---

### Step 2: Attach the Ad to a Button
1. Select the **Button** you want to play an ad (for example, the "Claim X2" or "Free Coins" button).
2. Attach the **`AdMobButtonHelper.cs`** script to it.
3. In the Inspector for that button, configure:
   * **Ad Type**: Set it to **`Rewarded`** (plays video for coins) or **`Interstitial`** (skippable popup).
   * **Coin Reward**: Set the amount of coins (e.g., `100` or `500`) the player gets for watching.

---

### How to Test It in Unity
* **Inside Unity Editor**: When you click the button, it will immediately print *"Mock Ad Showing"* in the console and give you the coins instantly (so you don't have to wait during development).
* **On Real Phone**: Once you import the AdMob SDK package and add `USE_ADMOB` in your scripting define settings, it will play a real video ad before giving the reward.
